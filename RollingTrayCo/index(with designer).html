From Our Workshop to Your Hands

The Rolling Tray Co was born from a simple idea: the tools we use should be as refined as the ritual itself. We are a small team of artisans dedicated to creating functional art. Each tray begins as a hand-selected piece of wood and is shaped, sanded, and finished with a passion for quality that you can see and feel.

Maintenance

A Guide to Caring for Your Tray

Published on 17th September 2025

Your hardwood rolling tray is a natural product, and with a little care, it will develop a beautiful patina and serve you for a lifetime. Follow these simple steps to keep your tray in pristine condition.

Routine Cleaning

For day-to-day cleaning, all you need is a dry or slightly damp cloth. Gently wipe away any residue after each use. Avoid harsh chemical cleaners or soaking the tray in water.

  • DO: Use a soft, lint-free cloth.
  • DON'T: Submerge the tray in water or use a dishwasher.

Deep Conditioning (Every 3-6 Months)

To keep the wood hydrated, we recommend conditioning it every few months with a food-safe mineral oil or a specialized wood conditioner. This will revitalize the grain and protect the wood.

  1. Ensure the tray is clean and completely dry.
  2. Apply a small amount of oil onto a clean cloth.
  3. Gently rub the oil into the wood, following the grain.
  4. Let the oil soak in for at least 30 minutes.
  5. With a new, clean cloth, buff off any excess oil.

Storage

When not in use, store your tray in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight or extreme changes in temperature to prevent fading or warping.

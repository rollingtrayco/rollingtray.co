From Our Workshop to Your Hands

The Rolling Tray Co was born from a simple idea: the tools we use should be as refined as the ritual itself. We are a small team of artisans dedicated to creating functional art. Each tray begins as a hand-selected piece of wood and is shaped, sanded, and finished with a passion for quality that you can see and feel.

Frequently Asked Questions

Where do you deliver?

We currently only deliver within the UK. We are working on expanding to international shipping in the near future!

How long will my order take to arrive?

We aim to dispatch all orders within 3 to 5 business days. We always strive to get your order to you as quickly as possible. Once dispatched, standard shipping times apply.

Are you releasing more designs or woods?

Yes! We are constantly prototyping new designs and sourcing unique, beautiful hardwoods. Follow our Journal for the latest updates on new products and limited editions.
